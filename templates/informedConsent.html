Study Title:
Towards Accessible Authentication for People with Dyslexia

Purpose of the study
The purpose of this study is to assess accessibility in authentication for dyslexic users and investigate how current authentication mechanisms can be made more accommodating in terms of design for the needs of dyslexic users. In this study we will explore alternative authentication mechanisms by considering a greater diversity of technologies, processes and opportunities to meet the needs of those for whom conventional alphanumeric authentication is challenging. We will perform rigorous people based research/comparative study to establish a secure authentication method which alleviates the difficulties dyslexic users experience with traditional methods of authentication
